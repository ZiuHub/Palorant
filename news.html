News

Stay updated with the latest announcements, events, patch notes, and stories from Palorant.

Game Updates

The all new Alyss Map: Vertical Challenges Await in Palorant

The latest map (the update of Abyss map), Alyss, introduces a thrilling battleground with perilous traps and vertical gameplay. Set in the secret stronghold of the Scions of the Hourglass, Abyss offers three lanes.

Agent Ability Adjustments: Enhancing Game Balance

In our ongoing efforts to maintain game balance, we've implemented adjustments to various agent abilities. These changes aim to refine gameplay dynamics and ensure a fair competitive environment.

Events

Palorant Championship 2025: Your Team in the Global Arena

Palorant proudly announces the 2025 Championship, a global tournament offering a total prize pool of $1 million. Teams worldwide are invited to participate and showcase their skills on the battlefield.

Alyss Exploration: Discover the New Map & Win Exclusive Rewards

To celebrate the launch of the Alyss map, Palorant is hosting the "Alyss Exploration" event from April 01 to May 31, 2025. Players who complete challenges on the Alyss map will earn exclusive rewards.

Patch Notes

Patch 9.08: Updates to Gunset Map and Introduction of Flinch Map

Patch 9.08 brings significant changes to the Gunset map, particularly in the B Site area, to enhance defense aspects and post-plant gameplay. Additionally, the new Team Deathmatch map, "Flinch," is now available.

Latest Map Rotation: Peas and Glimpse Return to Competitive Mode

In an effort to maintain variety and game balance, the map rotation has been updated. Starting with patch 9.08, the Peas and Glimpse maps return to competitive mode, while Icebow and Loxus have been rotated out.

Announcements

Palorant Community Art Contest: Calling Your Creativity

Palorant invites all players to participate in the 2025 Community Art Contest. This year's theme is "Agent's Journey." The best artworks will be featured on Palorant's official website and social media.

Developer's Blog: Vision and Future Plans for Palorant

The Palorant development team shares insights into the game's direction and future plans. Key focuses include adding new agents and maps, enhancing the anti-cheat system, and introducing more interactive features.
